About Us

Hello, My name is Carolyn Qura, and I am 6 years old. I love to bake cupcakes with my mom. My Mom bakes homemade regular, gluten free and cupcakes using a natural approach (no artificial flavor). Frosting is made with real butter, she never use Shortening. She use fresh fruits, real maple syrup, high quality spices, sea salt and pure extracts. No artificial sweeteners.

Our business is not only to sell delicious cupcakes but we like to say we're in the business of making lasting memories. We are honored to be a part of our customer's special moments and to thank them we strive to make everything as easy and smooth as possible. Our motto has always been to leave the world a little sweeter than we found it.

All of our cupcakes are baked fresh every day! You will never have a cupcake that has been refrigerated nor leftover from a previous day, this is our guarantee to you!
