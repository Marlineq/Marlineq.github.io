Perfect Vanilla Cupcakes/How to make moist vanilla cupcakes

Baking soda- 1/2 teaspoon

Baking Powder-1 teaspoon

Salt- 1/4 teaspoon

Egg- 2 Large

Milk-3/4 cup

Vanilla Essence-1& 1/2 teaspoon

Note: Powdering the sugar will make the creaming easy.

Bake it in a preheated ( preheat for 10 minutes)
